What's my story?

Starting out at the age of 14, I never stopped learning new computer programming languages and skills.
Early on, I began working with Java to make simple apps that I found interesting but within a month I knew this was exactly what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.
I was eager to learn more and expand my skills further.

I continued on to explore different areas of development in web, software, and scientific computing.
There was a great sense of satisfaction in learning different languages and gaining a new perspective each time.

My mission statement is to produce true utility through software.
