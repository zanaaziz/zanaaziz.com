What's my story?

Starting out at the age of 14, I never stopped learning new computer programming languages and skills.
Early on, I began working with Java to make simple applications that I found interesting.
Within a month, I knew this was absolutely what I wanted to do for the rest of my life.
I wanted to learn more and expand my skills further.

I continued on to explore various different fields such as mobile app development, web development, and scientific computing.
There was a great sense of satisfaction in learning these different languages and seeing things from different perspectives in doing so.

My mission statement is to produce true utility through software.
